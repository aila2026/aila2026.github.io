The Italian Association for Logic and its Application (AILA) organises every two years a meeting that gathers most of the Italian researchers studying mathematical, philosophical or computer science related aspects of logic. In 2026 the “Incontro di Logica” will take place in Rome (Italy). The meetings will feature plenary talks form leading international researchers, invited talks from the recipients of the AILA awards ("Paolo Gentilini" award, "Franco Montagna" award, "Ada Lettieri" award), and contributed talks. Contributions from researchers of any nationality are welcome.
